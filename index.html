About me

Hello, I’m Christopher Whalen and I’m a developer currently in training and the University of Denver’s Coding Boot Camp. I spent the last 14 years of my life in EMS as a Paramedic and am now making the transition into the Tech world. Paramedicine and software engineering surprising have multiple aspects in common. The Ideology of building a plan, applying that plan, encountering issues, then diagnosing and resolving of said issues are the primary aspects of both careers. My hope is to grasp a sound knowledge of computer coding and learn to apply it in the real world. --Tech industry, here I come!
